### WHAT DO YOU MEAN BY HACKATHON ?
-Definition: A hackathon is an event where individuals or teams collaborate intensively over a short period to create software, hardware, or business solutions. 
1
-Purpose: Hackathons aim to solve specific problems, promote innovation, and provide opportunities for networking and career development. 
1
-Format: They often involve a theme, judging criteria, prizes, and can be held in-person or online. 
1
-Benefits: Participants gain hands-on experience, develop new skills, and can showcase their work to potential employers. 
1
-Types: Hackathons can be general, skill development, or themed, catering to various interests and industries.
